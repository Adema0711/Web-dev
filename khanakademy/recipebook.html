Potatoes

  • Time: 1:30 hour
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Medium sized red-skinned potatoes 8
Good olive oil 1 tablespoon
Fresh rosemary 4 springs

Step 1: Preheat oven to 180 C.

Step 2:Stack the potato slices in the dish, quite tightly.

Step 3:Bake for 45 – 55 minutes, until the potatoes are golden and crispy around the edges.

Tomato salad

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe tomatoes 750 g
Small red or pink onion 1
Red wine vinegar 1/2 cup

Step 1:Peel and slice the onion into rings, about 1 mm thick.

Step 2:Slice the tomatoes in a variety of ways – wedges, cross wise, length wise, in quarters or halves.

Step 3:Drain the onion and add to the tomatoes along with the olive oil and the lemon zest.
